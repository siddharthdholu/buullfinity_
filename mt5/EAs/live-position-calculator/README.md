# 📊 Live Position Calculator MT5 EA

> Calculate the perfect position size in real-time based on your risk and stop-loss before placing a trade.

---

## Overview

**Live Position Calculator** is a lightweight and intuitive MetaTrader 5 Expert Advisor designed to help traders calculate the correct lot size instantly.

Instead of manually calculating position size every time you trade, simply enter your desired **Risk (%)** or **Risk Amount ($)** and define your **Stop Loss Price**. The EA automatically calculates the appropriate lot size in real time as the market price changes.

This ensures consistent risk management and saves valuable trading time.

# How It Works

The EA continuously monitors the current market price.

Whenever:

- Market price changes
- Risk changes
- Stop Loss changes

the position size is recalculated automatically.

No manual calculations are required.

---

# Using the EA

## Method 1 — Manual Stop Loss Price

### Step 1

Attach the EA to any chart.

---

### Step 2

Enter your desired risk.

You can use either:

- **Risk (%)**
- **Risk ($)**

Changing one field automatically updates the other.

---

### Step 3

Type your Stop Loss price into the **Stop Loss Price** field.

Example:

```
4066.74
```

---

### Step 4

The EA instantly calculates the correct lot size.

Example:

```
Position Size

4.49 Lots
```

---

### Step 5

Open the standard MT5 Buy/Sell order window.

Copy the calculated lot size.

Paste it into the **Volume** field.

Execute your trade.

---

# Method 2 — Drag & Drop Stop Loss

This is the fastest and most convenient method.

### Step 1

Click the **Drag SL** button.

---

### Step 2

A Stop Loss line appears on the chart.

---

### Step 3

Drag the line to your desired Stop Loss level.

---

### Step 4

The Stop Loss Price field updates automatically.

---

### Step 5

The calculated lot size updates instantly while dragging the line.

Simply copy the calculated lot size and place your trade.
